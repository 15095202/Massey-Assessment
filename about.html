The origin of Student Food:

Worldwide, people aged between 16-24 spend more money on food then any other demographic in the world.
These people are typically branded as 'millennials' and are typically students.
While globaly, tradtional cooking seems to be deminishing in this demographic, there is a wealth
of information, techniques and skills learnable in 5 minutes which will empower students to make healthy
nutritious and delicous food quickly and cheaply right on the internet. StudentFood aims to make this information
easily available, on the go on a phone or in the kitchen with easily comprehensable ingrediants lists, prices, and
recipes. With an accompanying youtube channel for visual reinforcement, StudentFood aims to be a hub of low cost deliciousness.
