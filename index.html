Welcome to Student food!

Student Food is a non profit, volunteer run blog designed for students! Student Food's aim is to get students in the kitchen cooking healthy, nutritious and delicious meals that are low cost and can be made in batches and saved. Here you will find tutorials on basic knife skills, what equipment is ideal and where to find it at a low cost,

All in one easy to reach, easy to navigate website!
